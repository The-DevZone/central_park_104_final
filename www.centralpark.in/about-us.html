About Central Park

Live the way 99.99% don't.

  • Home
  • About Us

Focused on providing a life extraordinaire, Central Park strives towards delivering an elevated experience. Our concept-based luxury residences with 5-star hospitality services make our residential properties most sought after.

We have inspired the real estate world with our commitment and values, and offered an opulent lifestyle like no other. Leading the luxury segment in Delhi-NCR, Central Park always transcends its preceding projects.

Milestones that inspire the future.
1970's
Early Years of Bakshi group

Foundation of a propitious Bakshi Group was laid with an aspiring vision for a better future in 1970s. The group functions across various business verticals encapsulating infrastructure, automotive & technology, and hospitality.

1982
Innovating with Baxy Engines

Since 1982, Baxy Engines has been leading the industry in quality and innovation. It combined advanced technology with engineering excellence to mark its vehement presence across three continents.

2001
Inception of Central Park

Embarked on the journey of creating the first luxury condominium. Central Park Golf Course Road commenced as an elite, green luxury residential development to provide resort-like lifestyle and luxury amenities.

2004
Launch of Central Park Bellevue

Built on the values of providing exceptional design woven into a living experience, Central Park Bellevue’s light-filled residences usher in green landscape and an Olympic-sized swimming pool.

2010
Launch of Central Park Resorts

Developed as coveted concept-based residences, Central Park Resorts was curated with the unique proposition of live-in-a-resort lifestyle where everything is at hand: An ornate club house, an ingenious concierge and five-star hospitality services that exalt your social life.

2011
Launch of St. Jerome

St. Jerome is committed to serving its residents with unsurpassed hospitality services. We ensure that our customers enjoy luxury in each detail. Imbibed with warmth and courteous approach, St Jerome invites the residents to explore the world of luxuries.

2014
Launch of The Room

Rooted in the expertise of providing world class amenities, The Room was launched with an idea of providing studio apartments in a five-star living space. It immediately struck a chord with the expatriate community and young corporate millennials.

2015
Launch of Central Park Flower Valley

A quintessential global township, nestled in the backdrop of Aravallis, Flower Valley believes in embracing nature. Established to give a feeling of spring throughout the year, comfort-seeking and self-indulgent king-size lifestyle coexists here with the incredible shades of green.

2015
Launch of Hyperdew

Providing integrated technological solutions for smart cities of India, Hyperdew was launched in 2015 to provide smart grade waste management system. It provides cost efficient waste management solution for cities and businesses to improve the environment.

2020
Footprint expansion on Dwarka Expressway

In a bid to expand further, Central Park set its foot on Dwarka Expressway by acquiring a 10-acre land parcel to establish its projects in the upcoming hub near Delhi Airport.

2021
Launch of Vedanya

To build a learning community which empowers individuals to become leaders of tomorrow, Vedanya spearheads change in education. The seven schools planned have a common vision: To raise a generation who will lead the future.

VISION

A vision that differentiates us

We endeavour to build the finest residential properties for future generations. Our designs are inspirational and reflect the essence of life at its best. We wish to nurture happiness in our creations!

We strongly believe that delivering projects is the first step in our long-lasting journey with our discerning clients, as our main contribution starts after possession of the homes. We aspire to provide clients with superior infrastructure, modern facilities that match their aspirations, a safe and secure living environment and a refreshing ambience with each project!

As part of our unflinching commitment to provide the finest experience, we consciously create homes infused with unparalleled hospitality to deliver outstanding quality of life. Every day we work towards achieving these goals!

Mr. Amarjit Bakshi
Chairman and Managing Director

He has been the key driving force in promoting Central Park and is credited for establishing brand 'Central Park'.

Central Park is a part of Delhi-based conglomerate Bakshi Group, which operates across Real Estate, Hospitality, Automotive, Infrastructure and Education sectors. Coming from humble beginnings to becoming a renowned industrialist in North India, his vision has propelled the group’s growth through strategic diversification across all business areas. Mr. Bakshi always believes in working for values as opposed to only monetary gains. He has been a recipient of numerous awards and honours over the past few years.
